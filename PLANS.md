## ğŸ” Content Sourcing Guidelines

### Quality Criteria
1. **Authenticity**: From reputable teachers, practitioners, or verified sources
2. **Relevance**: Applicable to modern life and challenges
3. **Clarity**: Easy to understand and actionable
4. **Impact**: Has potential to create positive change
5. **Diversity**: Represents various Buddhist traditions and perspectives

### Sources to Monitor
- Reputable Buddhist publications and websites
- Dharma centers and monasteries
- Verified teachers on social media
- Academic journals on Buddhist studies
- TED Talks and educational platforms
- Mindfulness and wellness communities
- Reddit communities (r/Buddhism, r/meditation, r/mindfulness)
- YouTube channels of authentic teachers
- Podcasts on dharma and mindfulness

### Red Flags to Avoid
- Content promoting superstition or magical thinking
- Commercial exploitation of dharma
- Cultural appropriation or misrepresentation
- Dogmatic or divisive teachings
- Unverified or anonymous sources
