- Transformation requires uncomfortable conditions

## ğŸ’­ Reflections for Difficult Times

When facing challenges, reflect on:

1. **What is within my control?**
   - Focus energy here
   - Let go of the rest

2. **What is this teaching me?**
   - Every difficulty carries a lesson
   - Stay open to learning

3. **How can I be kind to myself right now?**
   - Self-compassion is essential
   - Treat yourself gently

4. **Who can support me?**
   - Reach out to your sangha (community)
   - You don't have to face this alone

5. **What small step can I take today?**
   - Progress, not perfection
   - One moment at a time
