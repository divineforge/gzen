### Morning Practice (2 minutes)
- Start your day with **Metta meditation**. Place your hand on your heart and say:
  - *"May I be happy and peaceful."*
  - *"May I be healthy and strong."*
  - *"May I be safe from harm."*
  - *"May I live with ease."*

Then extend it outward:
  - *"May my loved ones be happy..."*
  - *"May even difficult people be happy..."*
  - *"May all beings be happy..."*

### Throughout the Day
- **Practice RAIN** when someone (including you) is struggling:
  - **R**ecognize what's happening (they're angry, you're stressed)
  - **A**llow it to be there without judgment
  - **I**nvestigate with kindness (what's underneath this?)
  - **N**urture with compassion (what does this person/I need?)

- **The "just like me" practice**: When someone irritates you, silently say:
  - *"This person wants to be happy, just like me."*
  - *"This person wants to be free from suffering, just like me."*
  - *"This person is doing their best with what they know, just like me."*

### Evening Reflection (3 minutes)
- Journal about one compassionate moment today:
  - *"When was I kind to myself today?"*
  - *"When did I show compassion to someone else?"*
  - *"Where could I have been kinder?"*

No judgment on the third questionâ€”just curiosity and learning.
