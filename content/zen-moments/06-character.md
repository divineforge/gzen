### Morning Practice (2 minutes)
- **Set your character intention** for the day. Choose one quality to embody:
  - *"Today, I practice honesty."*
  - *"Today, I practice patience."*
  - *"Today, I practice kindness."*
  - *"Today, I practice courage."*

Visualize yourself living this quality in your expected interactions.

### Throughout the Day
- **The Character Check**: Before important decisions or interactions, pause and ask:
  - *"What would the best version of me do right now?"*
  - *"Will I be proud of this choice tomorrow? In a year?"*
  - *"Does this align with my values?"*

- **Small Integrity Moments**: Practice in tiny ways:
  - Return the extra change the cashier gave you
  - Admit when you don't know something instead of bluffing
  - Keep promises to yourself (if you said you'd exercise, do it)
  - Speak truthfully even when a white lie is easier

- **The Virtue Journal**: At lunch or midday, jot down:
  - One moment where you acted with integrity
  - One moment where you could've done better
  - What you'll do differently next time

### Evening Reflection (3 minutes)
- **The Daily Review**: Ask yourself:
  - *"Did I live according to my values today?"*
  - *"Where did I compromise my character, and why?"*
  - *"What did I learn about myself?"*
  - *"Am I becoming someone I respect?"*
