### Morning Practice (2 minutes)
- **The Blessing Count**: Before touching your phone:
  - Name five things you're grateful for right now in this moment
  - Start with your body: "I'm grateful I can breathe, see, hear, move..."
  - Include simple things: your bed, the roof over your head, the new day
  - Feel it, don't just list it
  - This sets a grateful tone for your entire day

### Throughout the Day
- **The Gratitude Pause**: Set 3 random alarms on your phone
  - When it rings, stop whatever you're doing
  - Look around and find something to appreciate right now
  - *"I'm grateful for..."* (the person near you, the chair you're sitting on, the ability to rest, anything)
  - This trains your brain to notice blessings in real-time

- **The Appreciation Expression**:
  - Tell one person per day something specific you appreciate about them
  - Not generic: "You're great!"
  - Specific: "I noticed you always listen without interrupting. That means a lot."
  - Watch how it brightens both of you

- **The Complaint Flip**:
  - When you catch yourself complaining, pause
  - Find one related thing to appreciate
  - "Ugh, this traffic..." â†’ "I'm grateful I have a car and gas money"
  - Not to suppress feelings, but to add perspective

### Evening Reflection (5 minutes)
- **The Gratitude Journal**:
  - Write down three good things from today
  - Be specific: not just "my friend" but "when Sarah texted to check on me"
  - Include why it matters: "because it showed she was thinking of me"
  - Read your entries from previous days weeklyâ€”you'll be amazed what you've forgotten
